How to Use Smart Exam System

Quick guide for all users

🎓

Student

Register on Sign Up page with role Student. Wait for admin approval, then log in to access your exam dashboard.

Role → Student  |  Needs admin approval
👨‍🏫

Teacher

Register with role Teacher. Admin approves your account. Then log in to create exams and grade submissions.

Role → Teacher  |  Needs admin approval
🛡️

Administrator

Admin accounts are pre-created. Log in directly — no sign-up needed. Manage users and approve registrations.

Pre-registered  |  No sign-up needed
ℹ️

New User Steps

1. Click Create Account below  →  2. Fill details  →  3. Wait for approval email  →  4. Log in here!

1

Log In & View Dashboard

After approval, log in as Student. See enrolled courses, upcoming exams, and completed results.

2

Attempt an Exam

Click Available Exams. Start any published exam — answer MCQs, True/False, and essays within the time limit.

3

View Results

After grading, go to Completed Exams to see your score, percentage, and per-question AI feedback.

4

Track Progress

The Performance Trend chart shows your score history across all exams over time.

1

Create an Exam

Click Create Exam. Upload a PDF/DOCX file, select subject and difficulty — AI generates the paper automatically.

2

Review & Publish

Preview questions in the editor. Edit or delete as needed. Click Publish so students can attempt it.

3

Grade Submissions

MCQs are auto-graded. Review essay answers in Pending Grading. Use Grade All with AI for bulk grading.

4

Analytics & Leaderboard

Check Per-Course Analytics for pass rates. Use the Leaderboard to see top students per exam.

Upload a PDF/DOCX. The system extracts text and uses an AI model (Ollama/Flan-T5) to generate MCQ, True/False, and essay questions. You review and edit before publishing.

SBERT (Sentence-BERT) compares the student's answer to the model answer using semantic similarity. Answers below 75% confidence are flagged for teacher review.
